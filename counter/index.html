Counter App

Counter by Pulattech is a clean and simple app to help you count anything. It is designed to look great, save your phone’s battery, and be very easy to use.

Easy Features

  • Use Volume Buttons to Count Count without even looking at your screen. You can click the volume buttons on the side of your phone to count up or down.

  • Saves Battery The app keeps the screen dark except for the numbers. This uses very little battery while you are counting.

  • Lock Your Screen Lock the screen while counting so you do not click anything by mistake. A simple press and hold opens it again.

  • Gentle Vibration The phone gives a light buzz when you count so you know it worked. The screen also stays on so it won’t sleep while you count.

  • Keep Multiple Lists Track different counts at the same time. You can name them, color them, reset, or delete them when you’re done.

  • Set a Limit If you want to count up to a certain number, the app will vibrate to let you know you reached it.
