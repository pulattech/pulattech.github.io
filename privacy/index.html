Privacy Policy

Pulattech values your privacy. We believe that your personal data belongs to you.

Zero Data Collection

Our applications (including the Counter App) are designed with a privacy-first approach:

  • We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal information.
  • We do not use third-party tracking scripts, analytics tools, or advertising networks that monitor you across websites or apps.
  • All application data, settings, and counter logs are stored locally on your device. None of this data is ever transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

System Permissions

If our applications request system permissions (such as modifying system settings or enabling vibration), they are used solely to enable core features on your device:

  • Screen Brightness: Used dynamically in OLED dark mode to protect your screen and save battery while counting.
  • Haptic Feedback: Used to provide tactile confirmation when you increment a counter.

These permissions are never used to gather information or track your usage.
